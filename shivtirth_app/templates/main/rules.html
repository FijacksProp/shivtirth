Rules &
Regulations

SUPERVISION: Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult (18 years or older) at all times within the park premises.

ATTIRE: Guests with contagious diseases, open sores, or bandages are not permitted in the pools or attractions. If you have any health concerns, please consult with park staff before entering.

HEALTH & SAFETY: Proper swim attire must be worn at all times. Street clothes, including jeans and pants with buttons, are not permitted on water slides or attractions.

HEIGHT RESTRICTIONS: Some attractions have height and weight restrictions for safety reasons. Please check posted signs or inquire with staff for specific requirements.

DIVING: Diving is only permitted in designated areas. Please do not dive into shallow water or off of slides.

FOOD & BEVERAGE: Outside food and beverage are not allowed in the park. However, we have a variety of dining options available for your convenience.

ALCOHOL & DRUGS: The consumption of alcohol or drugs that impair judgment is strictly prohibited within the park premises.

QUEUE ETIQUETTE: Please respect others in line and refrain from cutting or saving spots. Line jumping may result in removal from the park without refund.

PERSONAL BELONGINGS: Lockers are available for rent to secure your personal belongings. The park is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

PHOTOGRAPHY & VIDEO: Photography and videography are permitted for personal use only. Commercial or professional equipment is not allowed without prior authorization from park management.

INCLEMENT WEATHER: In the event of lightning or severe weather, some attractions may be temporarily closed for safety reasons. Please follow instructions from park staff during such situations.

FIRST AID: If you require first aid assistance, please notify the nearest staff member immediately.

TICKET VALIDITY: Entry is restricted to one person per ticket, which is valid only for a specified date and time.

OPERATING HOURS: The water park operates from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

NON-TRANSFERABLE TICKETS: Tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.

SECURITY CHECK: All items are subject to a security check at the point of entry.

CONDUCT: Do not use foul language or engage in unsafe or offensive behavior. Show courtesy to fellow park guests and service staff.

SWIMWEAR REQREMENTS: Only Polyester or Nylon costumes (t-shirts, shorts, Bermudas, three-fourths, or swimsuits) are permitted on water rides.

PROHIBITED ATTIRE: Sarees, churidars/salwars, dupatta, formal pants, shirts, burkhas/pardas, school uniforms, jeans, cargos, and indecent attires are not allowed inside the water park.

SCARFS/SHAWLS: Scarfs or shawls are not allowed on rides for safety reasons.

SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS: Guests are advised to follow safety instructions displayed on boards. Management is not responsible for injuries/accidents caused by guest negligence.

NO DIVING: Diving into pools is prohibited as the pools are shallow.

HEALTH PRECAUTIONS: Pregnant women, heart patients, and those prone to epilepsy should avoid fast or adventurous rides.

PARENTAL SUPERVISION: Parents are advised to monitor their children closely.

RIDE RESTRICTIONS: Guests below certain heights and above specific weights may not be permitted on certain rides for safety reasons.

RIDE CLOSURE: The management reserves the right to close any ride at any time for safety or technical reasons without prior notice.

SMOKING: Smoking is prohibited in common areas of the park.

PETS: Pet animals are not allowed inside the park.
