Shivtirth Park Features

The ultimate water park experience with six exciting Features

Agro Tourism

Nestled amidst lush greenery and sprawling fields, Shivtirth offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Adventure Park

Shivtirth developed adventure aprk based on Mowgali jungle theme with 3D show and adventurous activities, nestled in a scenic valley of satpuda ranges.

Amusement Park

Whether you are seeking thrills or family fun, Nagpur's amusement park promises an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages!

Boating Park

Experience leisure and excitement with our elegant Shikara or engage in active fun with our Kayaks and Pedal Boats. There's a watercraft for every enthusiast, ensuring unforgettable moments on the water.

Waterfall Rappelling

This activity lets you descend down the face of a stunning waterfall, surrounded by the roar of rushing water and the beauty of a natural landscape.

Water Park

With a wide variety of attractions, top-notch amenities, and delightful dining options, this water park is the ultimate summer escape.

Mission & Vision

MISSION: To provide a transformative outdoor learning experience that connects children with nature and agriculture, fostering courage, confidence, and creativity through hands-on activities and educational programs.

VISION: To inspire the next generation to embrace environmental stewardship and agricultural knowledge, nurturing a deep appreciation for nature and empowering them to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
